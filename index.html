The 3-Step Process to Hire an AI

Most founders either ignore AI entirely or chase every hype cycle. Here's how to navigate the middle ground: evaluate honestly, hire strategically, and measure impact.

1

Audit Your Workflows

Before buying anything, understand what's actually broken in your business:

  • Which workflows waste the most time?
  • Where do errors creep in?
  • What's costing you revenue or margin?
  • What can only humans do well?

Output: A prioritized list of 3–5 workflows worth automating.

2

Design the Solution

For each workflow, decide: AI tool, in-house build, or hybrid?

  • Off-the-shelf: ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity (fast, cheap)
  • Productized AI: Zapier, Make, custom integrations
  • Custom build: When off-the-shelf can't handle it
  • Human-in-the-loop: Mix AI + human judgment

Output: A roadmap with tools, costs, and timelines.

3

Implement & Measure

Roll out methodically. Don't try to automate everything at once.

  • Start with 1 workflow, measure impact
  • Build confidence with quick wins
  • Document what works (and what doesn't)
  • Hand off to your team with clear playbooks

Output: A team trained on new AI workflows, measurable ROI, and a playbook for what's next.

The Goal: Not "we use AI for everything," but "we use AI where it actually works." ROI, not hype. Speed, not perfection.
