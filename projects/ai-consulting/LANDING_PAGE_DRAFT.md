**A:** No. We focus on integrating existing, battle-tested tools (Claude, ChatGPT, n8n, Make, etc.) into your workflow. If you need a custom model, we'll refer you to specialists.

### Q: How long does implementation take?
**A:** Most projects, 60–90 days. Some are faster. It depends on your complexity and how quickly your team adopts.

### Q: What if AI doesn't help our business?
**A:** Honest answer? Sometimes it doesn't. That's why we audit first. If we don't see clear ROI, we'll tell you upfront instead of selling you a solution you don't need.

### Q: Can you work with our existing tools?
**A:** Yes. We integrate with your current stack—Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Zapier, etc. We're not ripping and replacing.

**A:** We never send proprietary data to external AI services without your explicit approval. We prefer on-premise or private LLMs where feasible. Full data governance docs included.
