## Positioning Strategy

### Core Positioning
"**AI integration for founders who built their business without it.**"

Not "AI strategy consulting" (too vague).
Not "AI automation agency" (implies implementation by us forever).

**It's:** Hands-on partner who audits, implements, then hands off—so your team runs it.

### Key Messages

1. **We're Not Vendors**
   - We don't sell you software or lock you in

2. **We're Not Generic Consultants**
   - 15+ years shipping at scale (not bootcamp-trained)
   - We understand revenue, operations, and infrastructure
   - We speak the language of founders/operators

3. **We Move Fast**
   - 60–90 days to ROI
   - Minimal disruption to your team
   - Clear handoff by the end

4. **We're Honest**
   - If AI doesn't fit your business, we'll tell you

### Brand Voice
- **Tone:** Direct, confident, slightly irreverent
- **Avoid:** Jargon, hype, corporate-speak
- **Model:** Stripe's positioning (operator-focused, product-first, trust through clarity)

## Pricing Rationale

### Audit: $3,500 (1 week)
- **Comparable:** $150–200/hr consultant × 40 hours = $6k–8k
- **Our positioning:** Fixed fee = predictable, value-based
- **Low barrier:** Enough skin in the game to stay committed, low enough to attract trial

### Implementation: $12,000/month (12 weeks = $36k)
- **Comparable:** $200–300/hr × 400 hours (10 weeks) = $80–120k
- **Our pricing:** 30–45% discount for retainer + commitment
- **Client math:** Saves them $50k+ vs. hiring contractor, and we stay through handoff

### Support: $3–5k/month
- **Low end ($3k):** 1 integration, quarterly optimization
- **High end ($5k):** 3–4 integrations, monthly optimization + strategy
- **Comparable:** $150–200/hr × 20–30 hours/month = $3–6k
- **Margin:** Higher than implementation (pure profit post-delivery)
