# AI Consulting Outreach Sequence

## Overview
3-email sequence for cold outreach to SMB founders/ops leaders. Goal: Get 30-minute discovery call.

---

## Email 1: Hook (Day 1)

**Subject:** AI isn't the problem—integration is

---

Hi [Name],

Most founders I talk to have tried ChatGPT or Claude and thought "wow, that's powerful." Then they got stuck: how do you actually integrate this into your business?

I spent 15 years shipping at scale—payments at DoorDash ($35B company), engineering at Eight Sleep, product at Cornerstone. I've seen what works and what doesn't.

Right now, the biggest gap I see: companies know AI exists, but don't know how to deploy it without burning cash or disrupting their team.

That's what I help with.

**Would you be open to a quick 20-minute chat?** No pitch—just honest conversation about where AI could actually move the needle for [Company Name].

Either way, worth your time.

Cheers,
Caglar

## Talking Points for the Call

**If they ask "What do you actually do?"**

"I audit where AI creates real value in your operations, implement it with your team, then train you to run it independently. Typically takes 90 days. I handle the heavy lifting; you own the outcome."

**If they ask "How much does it cost?"**

"Depends on scope. Simple audit is $3.5k and takes a week. If you want implementation, it's $12k/month for 3 months. After that, most clients stay on $3–5k/month for ongoing optimization. But the audit tells us what actually makes sense for you."

**If they ask "What if AI doesn't help our business?"**

"Honest answer? Sometimes it doesn't. That's why I audit first. If I don't see ROI, I'll tell you upfront instead of selling you a solution you don't need."

**If they ask "Can you work with our existing tools?"**

"Yep. We integrate with your current stack—Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, etc. I'm not ripping and replacing."

## Metrics to Track

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **Emails sent** | 50/month | Phase 1 goal: 150 total |
| **Response rate** | 15%+ | If <10%, revisit subject lines |
| **Call booking rate** | 40% of responses | Aim for 6–8 calls/month |
| **Conversion (call → audit)** | 50–70% | First audit is critical |
| **Audit → implementation** | 60%+ | Shows positioning/fit |

---

## Cadence

**Month 1:**
- 50 cold emails (warm outreach priority first)
- Target: 6–8 discovery calls
- Goal: Close 2–3 audits

**Month 2:**
- 50 cold emails + follow-ups
- Target: 8–10 discovery calls
- Goal: 2–3 new audits + 1 implementation kicks off

**Month 3:**
- 50 cold emails + follow-ups
- Target: 8–10 discovery calls
- Goal: 2–3 new audits + 1–2 implementations in progress

---

## Red Flags (Don't Pitch)

- Client wants ongoing managed services (not your model)
- They're looking for "AI strategy consulting" with no implementation
- They have no budget or timeline
- They want you to build a custom AI model
- They're tire-kicking, not serious

**When to say no:** "Sounds like you need [different specialist]. I'll send you a referral. Let's stay in touch."

---

## Success Looks Like

After 3 months:
- 5–8 discovery calls booked
- 3–4 audits completed
- 1 audit → implementation conversion
- Case study / ROI proof point to use in future outreach
